====== Knucklebone Thugs ======
Created Sunday 17 March 2019

**XP: 1000**

5 Thugs

Tactics: Get close in to melee, pile on to one target at a time.
	- Send in two people after a damaging spellcaster if they can spare them
	- Once one thug is seriously wounded (down to 12HP or unconscious, as seems appropriate) they all Disengage and back off, firing crossbows as they go

**STATS:** 
Thugs: p 350 MM
